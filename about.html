About Us

7D Trade is a member of the most famous cryptocurrency trading community on the planet. A team of traders works 24 hours a day, seven days a week. One of the company's founders, Lee Hyuk, is connected to an international crypto trading house. Since working with the world's major exchanges for the past seven years, his team has extensive experience with blockchain technology. The firm is also home to its own in-house software engineers. The way people think about and use money is constantly changing. Currently, the existing financial system was built for a non-digital age, ignoring the needs of the modern individual and introducing unnecessary inefficiencies.

We can upgrade the world by reimagining the financial system with decentralized cryptocurrencies. The organization has developed its own signal for buying and selling bitcoin in key exchanges by our 7D Trade Team members manually on a regular basis. Our innovative technology, from trading platforms to the rest of the world, is used in the safest possible manner, and our firm sends signals to its investors so that they may earn greater and safer gains. We ensure a secure trade environment by providing industry-leading security.
